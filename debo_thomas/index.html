Contact Me

Debo Thomas Olushola stands as a versatile entrepreneur and visionary leader, boasting an impressive track record of over two decades. In his capacity as the Founder and CEO of Hastom, he has not only attracted more than $100 million in investments that have orchestrated a remarkable transformation in Ogbomoso, Oyo State to become the largest agricultural hub in Nigeria

Debo's leadership is characterized by a unique ability to identify potentials and seize unconventional opportunities, leading him to be the visionary force behind Valor City, a groundbreaking initiative known as Africa's first Human Development Smart City.

Outside work, Debo can be found on the golf course, swinging his clubs with a passion for the game and enjoying moments of relaxation with fellow golfers.
